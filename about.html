Our Structure:

Uralee is made up of three divisions.Each division delivers a wide range of complementary services, which we offer in a variety of combinations to suit the needs of our clients.

Engineers & Constructors (E&C):

E&C delivers EPC contracting (engineering, procurement, construction, installation testing and commissioning) services on a unit / lump-sum basis. Our services encompass both green field and brown field upstream developments – related Oil, Gas, Refinery, Energy & Power Sectors, (Oil & Gas Production. Pipeline, Storage & Export Facilities. Oil Refineries. Gas Liquid Projects. Petrochemical Projects. Renewable Energy Project. Non-renewable Energy Project. Power Transmission and Distribution Networks).

Edifice Construction (EC):

EC brings together integrated service for clients under flexible commercial models that are aligned with their requirements. Our projects cover upstream developments – related infrastructure projects, (High rise building, Desalination Project, Telemetry Systems, & Waste Water Treatment)

Civil & Structure (CS):

We are well versed in the construct of the various foundation for all types of static & rotary equipment’s, pipe bridges, pipe rack & pipe supports And Also construct of the Onshore Receiving/Launching pipelines & a platform tower Structure (Steel) with emergency platforms at intervals.

Mechanical & Piping (MP):

We are well versed in the Install, Testing & Commissioning of various piping, pipeline works which includes materials like CS, AS, SS & GRE / RTRP in above ground & below underground.
